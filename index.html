Selkies and Munros

A game of Snakes and Ladders in Java

Mainly a solo assignment and final project for my Introduction to Software Development module.
The goal of this project was to make a version of Snakes and Ladders called Selkies and Munros. Where the snakes were replaced with selkies and ladders with munros, functionally being the same as the well known and popular board game.
Initially, earlier in the semester, we were organised into groups and tasked to do the initial planning of the project. This went smoothly and allowed us to try working in groups in a software development oriented setting, where communication and organisation of the initial stages of the project were important. I played a more leadership role of organising people into doing different sections of the planning and potential UI design.
The rest of the project, including the programming, was done solo.
A lot of the major difficulty was breaking the game down into smaller chunks of tasks to code for. Being able to use previously completed code from your own programs helped as well, with everything being worked on little by little until the deadline grew closer. Managing these smaller tasks to be combined into a bigger program within the time limit was an invaluable lesson during this project.
